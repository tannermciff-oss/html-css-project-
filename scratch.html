The Grand Slam Tournaments

There are four major tournaments in professional tennis, each played on a distinct surface during different times of the year:

  1. Australian Open
    • Surface: Hard Court (Plexicushion)
    • Location: Melbourne, Australia
    • Time of Year: January
  2. French Open (Roland Garros)
    • Surface: Red Clay
    • Location: Paris, France
    • Time of Year: May
  3. Wimbledon
    • Surface: Grass
    • Location: London, England
    • Time of Year: July
  4. US Open
    • Surface: Hard Court (DecoTurf)
    • Location: New York City, USA
    • Time of Year: September
